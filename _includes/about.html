About

I work at the intersection of science and art, combining my creative skills with my in-depth knowledge of chemistry research. In addition to making illustrations, graphics and diagrams for academic publications, I am actively involved in scientific research itself. Scientists often struggle to communicate the abstract half-formed ideas brewing in their minds and by attempting to represent their thoughts in visual form, I generate a discussion that facilitates the refinement of new theories.

Although my specialism lies in the representation of complex scientific ideas and research in visual form, I am also very keen to applying my skills beyond this. Please get in touch using the contact sheet below if you would like my input on a project.
